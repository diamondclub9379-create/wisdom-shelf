*Feeling is the Secret* by Neville Goddard is one of the shortest and most powerful self-help books ever written. At just 28 pages, it can be read in a single sitting — but its core technique, practiced consistently, has the potential to transform every area of your life.

Published in 1944, this tiny book contains Neville's most distilled teaching: **the feeling of your wish fulfilled, impressed upon your subconscious mind, must produce the conditions that match it.**

## The Book's Structure

*Feeling is the Secret* has just four chapters:

1. **Law and Its Operation** — How the conscious and subconscious minds work together
2. **Sleep** — How the state between waking and sleeping is the gateway to creation
3. **Prayer** — How real prayer works (hint: it's not begging)
4. **Spirit — Feeling** — The deeper spiritual dimension of feeling

## Chapter 1: Law and Its Operation

Neville opens by explaining the relationship between your **conscious mind** (the "male" principle) and your **subconscious mind** (the "female" principle):

- The conscious mind **selects** the idea
- The subconscious mind **accepts and creates** it
- The subconscious does not judge — it accepts whatever the conscious mind impresses upon it
- The medium of impression is **feeling**

> "The subconscious is not selective; it is impersonal and no respecter of persons. The subconscious is not concerned with the truth or falsity of your feeling. It always accepts as true that which you feel to be true."

This is the key insight: your subconscious doesn't care if your feeling is "realistic." If you *feel* wealthy, it creates wealth. If you *feel* poor, it creates poverty. The feeling is the instruction.

## Chapter 2: Sleep

This chapter contains the practical technique. Neville explains that the moment just before sleep — when you're drowsy and your conscious mind is relaxing its grip — is the most powerful time to impress new feelings on your subconscious.

**The technique:**

1. As you lie in bed at night, **relax your body completely**
2. **Determine what you want** to experience
3. **Create a short scene** that implies your wish is already fulfilled
4. **Feel the scene as real** — engage all your senses
5. **Loop the scene** until it feels natural
6. **Fall asleep** while feeling the wish fulfilled

> "Sleep conceals the creative act while the objective world reveals it. In sleep man impresses the subconscious with his conception of himself."

What you feel as you drift off to sleep is what your subconscious works on through the night. This is why Neville considered the moments before sleep the most important of the entire day.

## Chapter 3: Prayer

Neville redefines prayer completely. Prayer is not asking God for favors. Prayer is **the psychological act of assuming the feeling of being and having what you desire.**

> "Prayer is the art of assuming the feeling of being and having that which you want."

When you pray "correctly" in Neville's framework, you don't beg or plead. You **give thanks** for already having received. You feel the gratitude, the relief, the joy of fulfillment. This is the only prayer that works because it's the only one that impresses the right feeling on the subconscious.

## Chapter 4: Spirit — Feeling

The final chapter connects Neville's practical technique to a deeper spiritual framework. He argues that **feeling is the secret of creation at every level** — from your personal life to the entire universe. Spirit (God, consciousness) creates through feeling. You, as an expression of Spirit, create through feeling too.

## How to Apply It Tonight

Here's a concrete example:

**Goal:** You want a promotion at work.

**Scene:** Imagine sitting at dinner with a close friend. Your friend says, "I heard about your promotion — congratulations! You really deserve it." You say "Thank you, I'm so excited about it." You feel the warmth, the pride, the relief.

**Practice:**
1. Get in bed tonight and close your eyes
2. Relax your body from toes to head
3. When you feel drowsy, begin the scene
4. Hear your friend's voice clearly
5. Feel the handshake, the smile
6. Loop the scene 5-10 times
7. Fall asleep *in* the feeling of already being promoted

**Tomorrow:** Go about your normal day. Don't look for signs. Don't tell anyone what you're doing. Just carry a quiet inner confidence that the promotion is yours. If doubt creeps in, gently return to the feeling.

**Persist:** Do this every night until it manifests. For some, it takes days. For others, weeks. The timing depends on how completely you accept the feeling.

## Common Questions

**Q: What if I can't visualize clearly?**
Feeling matters more than visual clarity. If you can *feel* the scene even without seeing it clearly, that's enough.

**Q: What if I fall asleep before finishing?**
That's actually ideal. Falling asleep in the middle of the scene means you're impressing it at the deepest level.

**Q: Can I work on multiple desires at once?**
Yes, but Neville recommends focusing on one at a time for best results, especially when starting out.

**Q: How long until I see results?**
There's no fixed timeline. The key variable is how completely you accept the new feeling as real. Doubt and impatience slow the process; faith and persistence accelerate it.
