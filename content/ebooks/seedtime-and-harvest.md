*Seedtime and Harvest* takes its title from Genesis 8:22 — "While the earth remaineth, seedtime and harvest shall not cease." Neville Goddard uses this principle to explain that **your imaginal acts are seeds that must inevitably produce a harvest** in your outer world. The only question is what seeds you are planting.

This book is uniquely rich in real-life testimonials from people who applied Neville's methods successfully.

## What You'll Learn

- How the law of seedtime and harvest operates in your daily life
- Biblical parables decoded as practical manifestation instructions
- The technique of revision — how to mentally rewrite past events to change their effects
- Real testimonials from people who manifested specific outcomes
- How to plant "seeds" of imagination that grow into desired results
- The importance of forgiveness and mental diet

*Seedtime and Harvest* stands out in Neville's catalog for its **abundance of real-world success stories**.
