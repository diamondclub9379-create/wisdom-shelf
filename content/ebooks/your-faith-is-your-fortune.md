In *Your Faith is Your Fortune*, Neville Goddard expands on his foundational teaching with a deeply mystical exploration of **how your beliefs literally shape your world**. Drawing extensively from biblical symbolism, Goddard reveals that the stories of Moses, Jesus, and the prophets are not historical accounts but blueprints for personal transformation.

This book is Goddard at his most poetic and profound, weaving together scripture, metaphysics, and practical instruction into a unified vision of human potential.

## What You'll Learn

- How "I AM" is the name of God — and why that matters for your daily life
- The mystical meaning behind key biblical stories and figures
- Why your current beliefs are the only thing limiting you
- How to consciously change your assumptions to change your life
- The relationship between faith, feeling, and manifestation

## Why This Book Matters

*Your Faith is Your Fortune* is considered one of Neville's most important works. It bridges the gap between spiritual mysticism and practical application, showing readers that the deepest spiritual truths are also the most practical tools for daily living. Essential reading for anyone interested in the intersection of spirituality and personal transformation.
