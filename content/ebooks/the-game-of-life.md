Florence Scovel Shinn's *The Game of Life and How to Play It* presents life as a game with spiritual rules — and teaches you **how to win**. Published in 1925, this short, punchy book explains how your words, thoughts, and beliefs create your reality, and provides specific affirmations and techniques for attracting prosperity, health, and happiness.

Shinn writes with a directness and warmth that sets her apart from other New Thought authors. Her examples are drawn from real-life situations, making abstract spiritual principles feel practical and immediate.

## What You'll Learn

- The four spiritual laws that govern life
- How your words and affirmations shape your reality
- Specific affirmations for prosperity, health, and love
- How to use intuition as a guide for right action
- The power of forgiveness and non-resistance
- How to overcome fear and negative expectations

## Why This Book Matters

Florence Scovel Shinn was one of the first female New Thought authors and her work remains **wildly popular nearly a century later**. *The Game of Life* has been cited as an influence by Louise Hay, Wayne Dyer, and countless modern manifestation teachers. It's the original "law of attraction" book — shorter, clearer, and more practical than most of its successors.
