*Feeling is the Secret* is Neville Goddard's most concise and practical book — a razor-sharp guide to the single most important principle in his entire teaching: **the feeling of your wish fulfilled is what makes it real**. In just four short chapters, Goddard explains exactly how your conscious and subconscious minds interact, and how to use this knowledge to transform your life.

This is the book that distills Neville's entire philosophy into its most actionable form.

## What You'll Learn

- The two functions of the mind: conscious (thinking) and subconscious (feeling)
- Why feeling — not thinking or visualizing — is the true creative power
- The critical role of the state between waking and sleeping (SATS technique)
- How to impress your subconscious mind with the feeling of your desire fulfilled
- Why your subconscious accepts whatever you feel to be true, without question

## Why This Book Matters

*Feeling is the Secret* is widely considered **Neville Goddard's most essential work**. At roughly 30 pages, it is the most efficient introduction to his method. The "SATS technique" (State Akin To Sleep) described in this book has become one of the most practiced manifestation methods in the world. If you read only one Neville Goddard book, this should be it.
