*The Power of Awareness* is widely regarded as **Neville Goddard's masterpiece**. In 27 focused chapters, Goddard lays out his complete system for conscious creation with unmatched clarity. The central thesis is simple but revolutionary: your awareness of being — what you assume yourself to be — is the cause of everything you experience.

This is Neville's most structured and comprehensive presentation of his ideas, making it the definitive statement of his philosophy.

## What You'll Learn

- The foundational principle: "Consciousness is the only reality"
- How assumptions harden into facts — and how to change your assumptions
- The difference between thinking FROM your desire vs. thinking OF your desire
- How to use attention and feeling to shift your state of consciousness
- Specific techniques for health, wealth, relationships, and self-transformation
- Why persistence in assumption is the key to manifestation

## Why This Book Matters

If *Feeling is the Secret* is Neville's most concise book, *The Power of Awareness* is his most complete. It covers every major aspect of his teaching in a clear, logical progression. Many consider it the **single best introduction to Neville's work** for serious students. The "Law of Assumption" concept described here has become one of the most discussed ideas in modern manifestation communities.
