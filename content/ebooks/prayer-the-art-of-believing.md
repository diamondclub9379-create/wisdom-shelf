In *Prayer: The Art of Believing*, Neville Goddard redefines prayer entirely. Prayer is not begging a distant God for favors — it is **the controlled use of imagination to create reality**. This book provides a systematic method for using the power of belief to bring about desired changes in your life.

Goddard draws on both mystical tradition and practical psychology to show that effective prayer is a skill that can be learned and mastered by anyone.

## What You'll Learn

- Why traditional prayer often fails — and what real prayer actually is
- The law of reversibility: how feelings create facts, not the other way around
- A step-by-step method for "praying" effectively through imagination
- How to maintain the feeling of your wish fulfilled despite contradicting evidence
- The role of belief, assumption, and persistence in manifestation

## Why This Book Matters

*Prayer: The Art of Believing* takes what might seem like a mystical concept and turns it into a **practical, repeatable method**. For anyone who has tried affirmations or visualization without results, this book explains what was missing — the element of genuine feeling and belief. It bridges the gap between spiritual practice and tangible results.
