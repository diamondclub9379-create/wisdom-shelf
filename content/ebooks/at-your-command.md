*At Your Command* is Neville Goddard's debut work — a short, electrifying introduction to his core teaching: **your imagination is God, and your assumptions create your reality**. Published in 1939, this brief book packs more transformative power per page than most self-help libraries.

Goddard argues that the Bible is not history but a psychological drama playing out within every individual. The "God" of scripture is your own wonderful human imagination, and by learning to direct it consciously, you can reshape every aspect of your life.

## What You'll Learn

- The revolutionary idea that imagination is the creative power behind all reality
- How biblical stories are allegories for states of consciousness
- Why "feeling is the secret" to manifesting your desires
- How to use your imagination to change your circumstances
- The relationship between consciousness, assumption, and experience

## Why This Book Matters

*At Your Command* is where it all began for Neville Goddard. Despite being only about 30 pages, it introduced ideas that would influence the entire New Thought and manifestation movement for decades to come. If you're new to Neville, this is the perfect starting point — short enough to read in one sitting, powerful enough to change your perspective forever.
