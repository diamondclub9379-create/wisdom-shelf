*Awakened Imagination* is Neville Goddard's elegant argument that **imagination is not fantasy — it is the most powerful force in the universe**. When your imagination "awakens," you realize it is not just a mental faculty but the divine creative power itself, operating through you.

Rich with literary references from Blake, Shakespeare, and the Bible, this book is Neville at his most eloquent and persuasive.

## What You'll Learn

- The difference between a "sleeping" and an "awakened" imagination
- How every great achievement in human history began as an act of imagination
- The secret of "entering into" the image of your desire
- Why most people use imagination destructively without knowing it
- How to consciously direct your imagination toward your goals
- Real-life success stories demonstrating these principles in action

## Why This Book Matters

*Awakened Imagination* is one of Neville's most beautifully written works. It makes the case that imagination is not an escape from reality but the very **engine of reality**. For creative professionals, entrepreneurs, and anyone who wants to understand the power of vision, this book provides both inspiration and practical methodology.
