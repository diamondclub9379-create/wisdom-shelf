## Our Mission

Wisdom Shelf is a free digital library dedicated to making **classic public domain books** accessible, beautiful, and easy to read. We believe that the world's greatest wisdom — from Stoic philosophy to timeless self-help — should be available to everyone, for free.

## What We Do

We take classic books that have entered the public domain and give them new life with clean formatting, thoughtful descriptions, and easy-to-download files in PDF and EPUB formats. No signup required, no paywalls, no tricks.

Our collection focuses on books that offer **timeless practical wisdom** — the kind of insights that were valuable centuries ago and remain just as relevant today:

- **Self-Help & Personal Development** — The original guides to mastering your mind and building a better life
- **Philosophy** — Ancient wisdom from Stoics, Greeks, and great thinkers
- **Business & Economics** — Classic texts on wealth, strategy, and leadership

## Public Domain

Every book on Wisdom Shelf is in the **public domain**, meaning the copyright has expired and the work is free for anyone to use. We source our texts from trusted repositories like [Project Gutenberg](https://www.gutenberg.org/) and verify the public domain status of every title we publish.

## Support Us

Wisdom Shelf is a passion project run by a small team. If you find our library useful, the best way to support us is to share it with others who love reading. You can also help by linking to us from your blog or website.
